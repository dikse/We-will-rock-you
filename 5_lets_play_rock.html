2. Meet the band before the beat!

Before you start playing with your wooden sticks, introduce your group and the band Queen.

Work with your group and make a basic presentation of the band.

Make a small poster with your group, a digital presentation or choose another format.

Your work can include: 

- The name of the group

- Country and city

- Type of music

- Group members and their instruments

- Famous songs.
