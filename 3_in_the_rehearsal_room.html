Dictionary

Bio-data

Definition:

It is the description of important facts about a person's life.

Descripción de hechos importantes sobre la vida de una persona.

Example:

My teacher asked me to write my bio-data, so I wrote my name, age, and what I like to do.

Mi maestra me pidió que escribiera mi biografía, así que escribí mi nombre, edad y lo que me gusta hacer.

Spanish word:

Datos personales, datos biográficos

This is how you can organize your team

How did your classmates feel?

Some classmates in your team might have felt nervous or scared.

Maybe someone helped them feel better.

Others might have felt angry because they didn’t know what to do.

When we work in a team, we can feel different things.

If we know our feelings, we can do the work better.

Here are some tips for the next team activity:

1. Think about how you feel.

2. Ask your teammates how they feel.

3. Talk to your team. Decide what to do to feel better and finish the work.

¿Cómo se sintieron tus compañeros y compañeras?


Algunos compañeros de tu equipo podrían haberse sentido nerviosos o asustados.

Cuando trabajamos en equipo, podemos sentir cosas diferentes.

Si conocemos nuestros sentimientos, podemos hacer el trabajo mejor.

Aquí tienes algunos consejos para la próxima actividad en equipo:

1. Piensa en cómo te sientes.

2. Pregunta a tus compañeros cómo se sienten.

3. I'm a fan of...

Choose a music group or singer you like.

You will create a short bio-data with this information:

1. Who are they? (Name)

2. Where are they from? (Country or city)

3. What is your favorite song?

4. Show part of the song: you can sing, dance, play it, or show a video or photo.

You can do your work in different ways:

Writing

Write your bio-data on paper or on the computer.

Drawing

Draw the band/singer or create a mini-poster.

Recording

Record a short video or audio.

With images or symbols

Use images or symbols if you need help with words.
