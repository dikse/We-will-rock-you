
Queen.
Queen was a British rock band formed in 1970.
They are considered one of the best
rock bands in recent history.
Freddie Mercury was the leader of the band
the singer and a songwriter.
He formed Queen in 1970
with Brian May and Roger Taylor.
Brian May was born in 1947.
He was the guitarist of the rock band.
John Deacon was the guitarist and songwriter,
he composed several singles for the group.
Roger Taylor was the drummer
and songwriter of the legendary band.
They composed famous songs like...
We will rock you, somebody to love,
I want to break free, Bohemian Rhapsody,
and we are the champions.
